welcome to Hindu Temple of Michigan

A temple is a sacred Hindu place of worship.
It represents the earthly home of Bhawan(the god), where one can visit to quiet the mind and experience spirituality. Visitors and devotees come to mandir(temple) to offer prayers before the murtis(), or sacred images, as well as to attend cultural classes and religious services known as sabhas. mandirs(temple) serve to both foster and further personal and collective worship. mandirs(temple) help to cultivate peace within communities and connect individuals to Bhagwan.
